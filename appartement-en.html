Beautiful family apartment with modern interior and central location

Located between the cross-country ski track and the Alpenrose practice lift, the Rhodania apartment building offers an ideal location. In summer, the ski track turns into a beautiful golf course, while the training slope becomes a children’s playground. Just a 30-second walk from both attractions in the center of Riederalp, Rhodania 55 is the perfect base for a ski or golf holiday.

The apartment is located on the first floor. Upon entering, you will find a hallway connecting the bedroom, separate bathroom, and living area. The open living room features a comfortable double sofa bed, television, dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. The living area provides access to a south-facing balcony with seating. From the living room, you can also access a separate bathroom with bath/shower and toilet. The bedroom has an en-suite bathroom with walk-in shower and toilet, and includes a double bed, wardrobe, and workspace.
